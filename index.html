Project Ulivar

Welcome to Project Ulivar!


The main use of this website will be to display info and ingame events. On top of this i'll also create small features like the a display for the research tree and battle calculators

Nations

Nations can come about in many ways. The most basic way is by tribes expanding.
Each nation will be led by one or multiple characters.

Their leadership and fame determines how the nation runs and how others see it.
The info each nation has can be seen in the tabs above.

Nation Administration

This contains general info about your nation.

  • Nation name : the name of your nation
  • Leader name : the name of your leader
  • Nation type : what form of nation it is. such as kingdom, tribe and others.
  • Culture : the culture your nation has
  • Religion : the primary religion of your nation

Nation Info

These determine a large amount of your nation and you'll need to keep an eye on these.
The higher amounts are the better.

  • Balance : the amount of money your nation possesses.
  • Pop : the amount of people living in your nation
  • Cities : the amount of cities in your nation
  • Governing : this shows how well your nation can govern itself. if it's low your nation loses stability

Pop is split into two types

  • City pop : These live in the cities and work more specialized jobs.
    They have a smaller impact on the governing capacity;
  • Spread out pop : These live in the country land.
    They provide food and basic resources for the cities around them and have a larger impact on governing capacity

Nation Status

This contains useful info that has larger effects on your nation.
These will be kept hidden for the player but will become visible using events that happen along the way.

  • Stability : how well your country is doing.
    The lower this is, the more likely a rebellion or civil war is.
  • War support : people don't like being at war.
    This determines how much your nation's opinion of it is right now

Nation Statistics

In here you can find all the info about how much a nation produces and owns.
The first few numbers are:

  • Taxation : How much each pop gives the state per week
  • Research : How many research points you gain per week
  • Civics points : How many civic points you gain per week

Then the nation's resources follow. These will always be 2 rows.

  • The first row : How much is gained per week.
  • The second row : How much they have in total.

After all that the districts follow.
This shows how many districts you have of each type.

Tribal Starts

For nations that do not start from other places there is the tribal start.
These will be split in a few types that players can choose from that align with their lore.
Each giving slightly different starter effects.

Default start 1 city with 8 pop (3 in the city and 5 spread out)
Fragmented 3 cities with 8 pop (2 in each city and 2 spread out)
Warring tribes 1 city with 8 pop (3 in the city and 5 spread out) with 2-4 similar tribes near each other
Nomadic 8 pop ( no cities)

Once you have decided on an idea you'll make a post in #char-n-nation-creation .
After that you'll have to wait to be vetted by a staff member.
Your post will look somewhat like this (but with more details)

Tribe name (tribal start) Retral tribe (warring tribes)
Description of tribe A tribe of dwarves that enjoy war
Description of start area Near the mountains and forest
(Extra) flag of tribe None
Any extra info None

Character Administration

This contains general info about your character

  • Character name : the name of your character.
  • character title : what title does your character hold. is it hero or Warking. anything goes.
  • Character Race : as we live in a fantasy world many types of humanoid and inteligent life forms exist. what will your character be.
  • Type : Wht type is your character these are: Leader, Merchant, religious figure, admiral, general, normalfolk.
  • character Player : The player who leads this character currently.
  • Culture : the culture your character is from.
  • Nation : what nation does your character originate from. this will be displayed here. If this contains N/A it means the character doesn't reside in one nation primarily. if it contains (exciled) the character not accepted by their nation.

Character status

these contain the general wellbeing and status of your character. these will have a mayor impact on your character and their ability in roleplay.

  • Age : how old is your chartacter.
    Very young or absolutely ancient. Younger characters have less chance to get ill and heal faster, but they have generally lower stats then their older counterparts.
    Older characters starting from 60 has a chance to die of old age each week. Pre-existing conditions that they may have increase this chance. their old age however has given them vast knowledge on their subject and have higher scores.
  • Status: the status determines how the character is doing. The possible statuses are:
    • Alive : your character is fully healthy and operational.
    • Wounded/ill : your character has fallen ill or gotten wounded from their daily life. They can still do their tasks but have to be more carefull.
    • Bedridden : No longer being able to go outside the character can't preform their tasks anymore. they might be close to death from this.
    • Death : your character has died. They will be removed from the active character list and moved to another page for all death characters.
